Grilled Salmon
Tap to view
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Fresh parsley
  • Lemon slices
  1. Season the salmon fillets with salt, pepper, and paprika
  2. Heat olive oil in a grill pan over medium heat.
  3. Grill the salmon for 4–5 minutes on each side until cooked.
  4. In a small pan, melt butter and sauté garlic for one minute.
  5. Add lemon juice and stir well.
  6. Pour the lemon butter sauce over the grilled salmon.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve with lemon slices.

Sayadiyah Fish
Tap to view
  • 1 whole fish (hamour or sea bass) – 1 kg
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups water
  • Lemon slices (for serving)
  1. Clean and season the fish with salt and spices.
  2. Fry the fish in oil until golden brown, then set aside.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  4. Add tomatoes and spices, then cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add rice and water, then simmer until the rice is cooked.
  6. Serve the fish on top of the rice and garnish with lemon slices
