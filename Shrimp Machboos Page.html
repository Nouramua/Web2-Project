Shrimp Machboos

Masterfully created by Chef Saqr Al-Hamed

Category

Arabian Gulf Coast Cuisine

Description

Shrimp Machboos is a traditional Gulf seafood dish made with shrimp and aromatic spiced rice. It is widely popular in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia and represents the culinary identity of the Arabian Gulf coast.

45m Total Time
Medium Level
850 Cals

Ingredients

  • Shrimp (Cleaned) 500 g
  • Basmati Rice 2 Cups
  • Onion (Chopped) 1 Med
  • Garlic (Minced) 3 Cloves
  • Tomato Paste 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric & Cumin 1 tsp each
  • Cardamom & Pepper 1 tsp each
  • Shrimp Stock/Water 3 Cups

Instructions

1

Wash and soak the rice for 20 minutes.

2

Heat oil in a pot and saut√© onion and garlic until fragrant.

3

Add tomatoes, tomato paste, and spices, then cook for a few minutes.

4

Add shrimp and cook briefly until they turn pink.

5

Add shrimp stock and bring to a boil.

6

Add rice and cook until tender and the liquid is absorbed.
